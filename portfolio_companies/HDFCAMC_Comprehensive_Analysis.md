## SECTION 1: BUSINESS OVERVIEW

### 1. Business Model

HDFC Asset Management Company is India's largest active mutual fund manager with a comprehensive investment management platform:

**Revenue Streams:**
- **Management Fees (70%+):** Fee charged on AUM - 0.5-2.5% depending on fund category
- **Performance Fees (5-8%):** Success-based fees on select schemes
- **Advisory & PMS (15-20%):** Portfolio management, alternative investments
- **Distribution Income (5%):** Third-party product distribution

**Customer Segments:**
- **Retail Investors:** 15.4 million unique investors (26% of industry's 59 million)
- **Institutional Clients:** Corporates, pension funds, insurance companies
- **SIP Investors:** Largest SIP book in industry (structural moat)

**Product Mix (as of Q3 FY26):**
- Equity Funds: 65.5% of AUM (highest among large AMCs, high margin)
- Debt Funds: 20.1% of AUM
- Liquid Funds: 8.3% of AUM
- Others (Hybrid, Commodities): 6.1% of AUM

**Geographic Presence:**
- Pan-India: 210 branches across 200+ cities
- 75,000+ empanelled distribution partners
- Strong presence in B30 cities (30%+ AUM from beyond top 30 cities)

**Market Size:** India MF Industry QAAUM ₹77.1 Lakh Crore (Sep 2025), growing 18.4% CAGR
**Growth Drivers:**
- Low MF penetration (AUM/GDP 16% vs 100%+ developed markets)

- SIP culture (₹25,000+ Cr monthly inflows, rising)

- Young demographics + rising disposable income

**Competitive Intensity:** MEDIUM-HIGH

**Regulatory Environment:**
